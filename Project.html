Glazed Lemon Pound Cake Loaf


Perfectly lemony and moist loaf, with a tart sweet lemon glaze. Freezes beautifully




My go-to lemon loaf recipe for years! This delicious Glazed Lemon Pound Cake Loaf is always Perfectly moist and lemony!

I have been making this same lemon loaf for years. It's my "go to" when only lemon loaf will do. The fact that I've never posted it to this point is simply my belief that maybe the internet didnt really need another lemon loaf recipe. But what the heck. It's good,it's tried and tested (many,many times!) and for anyone who has yet to find their"go to" lemon loaf, it's definitely worthy of consederation.
